Indoor Air Quality

  • What is Indoor Air Quality (I.A.Q.) ?

    The quality of air we breathe inside our office, house and car can be broadly termed as Indoor Air Quality.

  • Why is I.A.Q. important?

    Because we spend 90% of our working time inside our office, breathing in the air coming out of the Air Conditioning Ducts. Unlike outdoor air, indoor air is recycled continuously causing it to trap and build up pollutants

  • What we do?

    We clean and disinfect the AC Ducts.
    Duct cleaning is done starting from the AHU side. Starting at the supply end, running into each branch and up to the end of the duct.

  • Why is it needed?

    By having your air ducts cleaned periodically, the indoor air quality in your workplace enhances, improving the quality of air. Also, the efficiency of your Air Conditioning system improves, reducing the energy consumed!

Why Duct Cleaning?

  • To remove all the construction debris, dust, dirt, moulds, bacteria, VOC, etc from inside the duct
  • The above ensures that the air that passes through the duct will be clean and healthy
  • Reduction in power consumption as cleaner ducts mean better and quality air at the required place

Benefits of Duct Cleaning & Disinfection

  • Reduces Allergens and Irritants
  • Creates a Cleaner Living Environment
  • Helps Everyone Breathe Easy
  • Removes Unpleasant Smells and Odours from your office
  • Improves Air Flow Efficiency
  • Reduced the power consumption of your Air Conditioners
